A. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

This Product is a yield enhancement product. The Redemption Amount at the end of the term is determined based on whether the Final Price of the Underlying is above, at, or below the Strike Price. The principal is not protected.

Product Features:

  1. Very short tenor and flexible. Different strikes can reflect different market view.
  2. Potential high returns on the investment.
  3. Buying cryptocurrency at a discounted price. The Investment Currency can potentially be converted into the Target Currency at a discounted price.

Possible Payoffs of the product:

At maturity, in the event that the Final Price of the Underlying closes at or above its Strike Price, the Product will be redeemed in its Investment Currency with high returns. In the event that the Final Price of the Underlying closes below its Strike Price, the Product will be settled in the Target Currency at the Strike Price together with a return, which is at a discount to the Initial Price.

 

 

 

 

Scenario Analysis: Sample Terms

 

Scenario 1:

On the Maturity Date, the Final Price of the Underlying (102%) is higher than its Strike Price (90%):

 

The Product Investor will receive BTC and the BTC return is calculated as follows:

10*(1+0.877%) = 10.0877 BTC

Scenario 2:

On the Maturity Date, the Final Price of the Underlying (88%) is lower than its Strike Price (90%):

The Product Investor will receive ETH and the ETH return is calculated as follows: (10/0.07245)*(1+0.877%)=139.2367 ETH

Effectively, the Product Investor converts the BTC into ETH at the Strike Price (90%). Compared to the Initial Price, the Product Investor receives a 10% discount and more ETH.
