A. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

This Product is a yield enhancement product. The Product may be redeemed prior to maturity under certain conditions. The investment principal is not protected.

PRODUCT FEATURES:

  1. Attractive coupon: The Product can potentially provide attractive returns.
  2. Callable feature: This feature is triggered if the underlying digital currency closes at or above the callable prices on specified observation dates and has not dropped below the Knock-in Price. Clients may potentially benefit from this feature which works to shorten the holding period of the Product.
  3. Attractive returns in range-trading markets: Appropriate for Clients who are either moderately bullish or who hold a “range-trading” view regarding the underlying digital currency.

SCENARIO ANALYSIS:

Sample Terms

Explanation of Payout Terms:

  1. If the price of the Underlying Digital Currency is at or above the Callable Price on any Autocallable Observation Date and a Knock-in Event has not occurred, the Client is entitled to immediately receive 100% of the Investment Amount, together with the Bonus Coupon in the Investment Currency.

  1. If there is no Autocallable Event and a Knock-in Event has not occurred prior to the Final Date, the Client is entitled to, at maturity, receive 100% of the Investment Amount, together with the Bonus Coupon in the Investment Currency.

  1. If there is no Autocallable Event and a Knock-in Event has occurred, and the Final Price is at or above the Conversion Price, the Client is entitled to receive only 100% of the Investment Amount in the Investment Currency.

  1. If there is no AutoCallable Event and a Knock-in Event occurs at any time, and the Final Price is below the Conversion Price, the Client is entitled to receive the Underlying Currency, in a number calculated as the Investment Amount divided by the Conversion Price.

Scenario 1:

On an Autocallable Observation Date, price of the Underlying Digital Currency is at or above the Callable Price.

· On the 3rd Observation Date, price of the Underlying Digital Currency (107%) is above Callable Price (105%). On this observation date, product can be redeemed immediately.

· Client is entitled to receive 100% of the Investment Amount and a Bonus Coupon at 30% p.a. in USDT.

Scenario 2:

The price of the Underlying Digital Currency is below the Callable Price on all the Autocallable Observation Dates, but is above the Knock-in Price at all times, and is above the Callable Price on the Final Observation Date.

  • There is no early redemption of the Product.
  • On the Final Observation Date, Underlying Price (109%) is above Callable Price (105%). The Client is entitled to receive 100% of the Investment Amount and the Bonus Coupon of 30% p.a. in USDT.

Scenario 3:

The price of the Underlying Digital Currency is below the Callable Price on all the Autocallable Observation Dates but is above the Knock-in Price at all times.

  • Prior to the Final Observation Date, there is no early redemption and the price of the Underlying Digital Currency does not fall to or below the Knock-in Price at any time.
  • At maturity, the Client is entitled to receive 100% of the Investment Amount and the Bonus Coupon of 30% p.a. in USDT.

Scenario 4:

There is no early redemption and a Knock-in Event has occurred. The Final Price is at or above the Conversion Price.

  • Prior to the Final Observation Date, there is no early redemption and the price of the Underlying Digital Currency has fallen below the Knock-in Price.
  • At maturity, the Final Price is at or above the Conversion Price, the Client is entitled to receive 100% of the Investment Amount in USDT.

Scenario 5:

There is no early redemption, but a Knock-in Event has occurred. The Final Price is below the Conversion Price.

 

  • Prior to the Final Observation Date, there is no early redemption and the price of the Underlying Digital Currency has fallen below the Knock-in Price on the Final Observation Date.
  • At maturity, given that the price of the Underlying Digital Currency is below the Conversion Price, the Client is entitled to receive a number of BTC equal to the Investment Amount divided by the Conversion Price.
